The Signs That The Door Spring Broke?

Various signs indicate that your garage door spring is out of service. The first sign is when the cables are loose. Secondly, the door is heavy while operating. Thirdly, there’s a gap in the spring that wasn’t there before. Fourthly, your garage door closes with a loud bang. Another sign indicating a problem is that your door doesn’t close fully.


When you notice any of these signs, it is best not to come near the door or try to fix it yourself. The consequences are severe, so it’s better to first disconnect the opener. Then, to call Overhead Door, Plano, TX, and ask for emergency service, and we will deliver.
